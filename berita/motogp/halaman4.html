Davizioso: the attedance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 staright years - the third highest on the grid behind Valentino Rossu (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent Challenger.
